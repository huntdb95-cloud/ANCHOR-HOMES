About Anchor Homes LLC

Thoughtfully crafted homes, delivered with care — rooted in integrity and long-standing experience in residential construction.

Meet the Owner

Anchor Homes LLC is led by Austin Bream, a Pennsylvania native with a decade of hands-on construction experience and a proven track record of leading teams in residential homebuilding.

Austin has spent over 10 years in construction, including 5 years as head of construction for a large home builder. During that time he oversaw ground-up builds, complex remodels, and detailed finishes, learning how to deliver consistent quality while keeping projects on schedule and on budget.

Anchor Homes LLC was founded around a simple idea: do what you say you’re going to do, and take care of people along the way. That means clear communication, honest expectations, and a commitment to standing behind the work long after the job is done.

What Anchor Homes Stands For

Every project is guided by a few core principles that shape how we build and how we serve our customers.

Integrity first

We believe in doing the right thing even when no one is looking — from how we price jobs to how we handle surprises on site.

Customer-focused

Clear communication, regular updates, and respect for your time and budget are built into every stage of the project.

Craftsmanship & detail

From framing to finishes, we work with trades and materials that reflect the level of quality we’d expect in our own homes.

Long-term relationships

Our goal is to be the builder you call back for the next project — and the one you feel confident recommending to family and friends.
