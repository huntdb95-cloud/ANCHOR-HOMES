About Anchor Homes LLC

A construction company grounded in experience, straight talk, and doing right by homeowners.

Meet the Owner – Austin Bream

Anchor Homes LLC is led by Austin Bream, a Pennsylvania native with a decade of hands-on construction experience. Austin spent over ten years working in the field, learning the trades, understanding jobsite realities, and seeing firsthand what makes a project run smoothly—or fall behind.

Before launching Anchor Homes LLC, Austin served for five years as head of construction for a large home builder. In that role he oversaw scheduling, quality control, trade coordination, and customer walk-throughs across a wide mix of projects. That experience built a deep understanding of how to deliver consistent craftsmanship while keeping budgets and timelines in check.

Today, Anchor Homes LLC focuses on a simple foundation: integrity, clear communication, and taking care of the customer. That means accurate expectations up front, honest conversations when decisions need to be made, and a steady focus on the details that make a home feel solid for years to come.

How we work

  • Transparent budgeting and scope discussions.
  • Regular check-ins and walk-throughs with homeowners.
  • Trusted subcontractors held to clear quality standards.
  • Job sites kept organized and respectful of your property.

What you can expect

  • Real timelines and realistic options, not overselling.
  • Attention to the structure you can’t see, not just the finishes.
  • Responsiveness when questions come up during and after the job.

Whether you’re planning a new build or a significant renovation, Anchor Homes LLC approaches your project with the mindset that it should feel good not just on the day you move in, but ten years later.
