Subcontractors Agreement

This document is a binding contract, which will serve as a blanket agreement for and between Anchors Homes LLC, herein known as Builder, and the undersigned, referred to hereafter as Subcontractor. By signing, Subcontractor and Builder agree to the terms set forth herein. This agreement shall remain in force from the date hereof and from year to year, unless a change is agreed to in writing by both Builder and Subcontractor.

1. General Performance

All work of the Subcontractor will be performed in a good and workmanlike manner in accordance with the plans and specifications for each job, and must comply with all Federal and State laws, codes, and regulations, and all county and/or municipal ordinances and regulations effective where the work is to be performed under this contract. All permits, fees, taxes, and expenses connected with such compliance are to be paid by the Subcontractor.

2. Independent Contractor

Builder and Subcontractor agree that the Subcontractor is being hired solely as an independent contractor, and that neither the Subcontractor nor Subcontractor’s employees shall be deemed to be employees of the Builder.

3. Time

Subcontractor agrees to promptly begin work as soon as notified by Builder, and to complete the work in a professional and workmanlike manner within a reasonable period once work is commenced, and in any event by the deadlines established by Builder in writing. Subcontractor shall cooperate with other trades so that each may reasonably complete their respective work within required time frames. Subcontractor shall provide competent supervision, sufficient skilled workers, and adequate materials to maintain Builder’s work schedule, and warrants that all required licenses and permits are in place. If Builder determines that Subcontractor’s work does not conform to the drawings, specifications, or quality standards, Builder may require correction; if Subcontractor does not correct such defects on Builder’s timetable, Builder may correct them and charge back the cost to Subcontractor.

4. Extras

No deviations from the work specified in the contract will be permitted or paid for unless a written extra work or change order is first agreed upon and signed as required.

5. Assignment

No assignment of this subcontract agreement by Subcontractor is permitted without prior written permission from Builder.

6. Hold Harmless

Subcontractor agrees to protect, defend, and indemnify Builder against, and hold Builder harmless from, any and all claims, demands, liabilities, losses, expenses, suits, and actions (including attorney’s fees) arising out of, or alleged to arise out of, the work covered by this subcontract, including injury, death, or property damage, even if attributable in part to negligence or other fault of Builder or its officers, agents, or employees, except to the extent it is determined that the injury, death, or damages were attributable solely to Builder’s fault or negligence. Builder may withhold from payments due such amounts as may be reasonably necessary to protect against such liability.

7. Mechanics Lien

Subcontractor shall furnish all partial and final lien waivers and sworn statements for Subcontractor and Subcontractor’s material suppliers as required by Builder, as a condition precedent to partial and final payments. If any lien, encumbrance, or claim for nonpayment is asserted against Builder’s property arising out of this contract, Subcontractor shall protect, indemnify, hold harmless, and defend Builder from and against such liens and all related costs and expenses, and Builder may treat such assertion as a default and back charge Subcontractor.

8. Clean-Up

Subcontractor agrees to clean up all debris, trash, and refuse generated by Subcontractor’s trade at the end of each day and deposit it in the trash bin provided by Builder, leaving the job broom-clean for the next trade. Failure to comply after notice may result in Builder performing the clean-up and back charging Subcontractor.

9. Default

If Subcontractor defaults in the performance of any duties or obligations hereunder and such default continues after verbal or written notice, Builder may immediately terminate this Agreement. Subcontractor shall be due only such sums for approved work up to termination and shall furnish lien waivers upon termination and payment.

10. Care of Materials

Subcontractor agrees to properly care for materials supplied by Builder, store usable materials in an orderly way protected from damage, and properly dispose of non-usable materials. Builder may hold Subcontractor accountable and back charge for materials damaged by negligent care. Subcontractor shall promptly notify Builder of any defects in materials supplied by Builder.

11. Payment

Builder shall provide stated time frames for Subcontractor to submit invoices. Invoices not received by the stated time will be processed in the next pay period. Invoices in question may be held in their entirety until any disputed charge is resolved, and payment for a disputed charge may be held from Subcontractor’s total payment regardless of the specific project in dispute.

12. Insurance

Subcontractor shall provide Builder with a Certificate of Insurance showing required coverage in effect during the period of the contract, including Commercial General Liability, Workers’ Compensation (as applicable), and Automobile Liability with specified minimum limits, naming Builder as Additional Insured where required. Subcontractor agrees to notify Builder immediately of any changes, cancellation, or non-renewal of coverage.

Health and Safety

Subcontractor agrees to exercise all precautions necessary to prevent accidents and to comply with all applicable health and safety requirements, including OSHA and Tennessee OSHA. Subcontractor is responsible for any penalties for non-compliance by Subcontractor, its employees, or agents, and for proper handling and disposal of any hazardous materials.

13. Conduct

Subcontractor, its employees, and agents shall conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times. The use of, or being under the influence of, alcohol or drugs on the jobsite is prohibited. Subcontractor agrees that, in the event of any accident on the jobsite where Subcontractor personnel are present, such personnel may be required to submit to drug testing under Builder’s policy. Subcontractor further agrees not to enter into any agreement with Builder’s customer regarding the property while the house is under construction and until the customer has purchased the property.

14. Maintenance of Erosion Control

Subcontractor agrees not to disturb erosion control systems constructed on site on behalf of Builder. If silt fencing or other erosion control measures are moved for access, they shall be returned to effective status immediately. Subcontractor shall indemnify and hold harmless Builder from all claims, fines, damages, losses, and expenses arising from damage to erosion control structures caused by Subcontractor, its employees, or agents.

15. Arbitration

Any dispute respecting the provisions of this Agreement or the meaning of drawings or specifications shall be decided by binding arbitration as the sole remedy. Each party shall select one disinterested arbitrator, and those two shall select a third. The decision of a majority of the arbitrators shall be binding, final, and conclusive. The expense of arbitration shall be borne equally by Builder and Subcontractor.

16. Warranty

Subcontractor shall warrant against defects in workmanship and materials supplied by Subcontractor for a period of one year from the date the home is first occupied by the homeowner. The warranty shall be of the same standards and duration as Builder’s warranty to the Owner.

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read and agree to the terms of this Subcontractors Agreement.
